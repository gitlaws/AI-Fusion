AI Fusion

AI Fusion is a tool showcasing my ability to integrate AI assistant bots, powered by advanced LLM models and customized into your software and web applications. Over the coming decade AI in the workplace has the potential to transform industries and substantially augment productivity across a variety of applications.

Features

  • Personalized AI assistants tailored for specific industries or tasks
  • Enterprise solutions, web & mobile apps, specialized software
  • Customized to meet the unique needs of your business
  • Utilizes RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) that can answer user questions in various contexts by cross-referencing internal and external data sources

RAG Use Case

In today's traditional admin panels users often manually navigate through complex databases, searching for specific information. Imagine effortlessly searching through countless records, by simply asking the AI assistant, "How many new user accounts do we have?" Or, any specific information, from internal or external databases and the assistant will instantly provide accurate responses of your company's data sources.
